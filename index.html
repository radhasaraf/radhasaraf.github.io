Radha Saraf

I am a masters student in the Robotics Engineering prorgam at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI). I am currently working at the PeAR(Perception and Autonomous Robotics) lab under the guidance of Prof. Nitin Sanket.

Previously, I worked at Skylark Drones, where I was developing pipelines for solving computer vision problems associated with drone surveys. I was also the primary backend developer for Drone Mission Ops, the proprietary software for managing commercial drone operations.

I obtained my bachelors in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from VNIT(India) in 2018, where I worked on various projects as part of the robotics club, Ivlabs.

I am interested in the areas of motion planning & perception.

Probability based edge detection
project page

Developed a simplified version of pb, which finds boundaries by examining texture and color discontinuities in addition to intensity discontinuities across multiple scales.
